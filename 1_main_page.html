Navigating Green Chengdu

Green Space Accessibility

Chengdu's ‘Park City’ programme aims to integrate green space into the city's development, increasing residents' access to nature and public recreational areas. While green space is a key feature of urban sustainability, its accessibility varies in different parts of the city.

This interactive map explores park accessibility in Chengdu, examining how well green spaces are connected by walking, cycling, and public transport within 5, 10, and 15-minute travelling distances. By visualising the accessibility model, the project provides an exploratory tool to assess changes in green space connectivity in the city.

Use the map to explore how different neighbourhoods connect to the park and see how accessibility is changing in Chengdu.
